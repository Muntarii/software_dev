Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredient Quantity
Spaghetti 200g
Ground Beef 500g
Tomato Sauce 2 cups

Details

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 4

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Brown the ground beef in a pan.
  3. Add the tomato sauce to the beef and simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. Combine the sauce with the spaghetti and serve hot.

Classic Pancakes

Ingredient Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Eggs 2

Details

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Cook Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl until smooth.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour batter and cook until bubbles form, then flip.
  4. Serve with syrup and butter.

Caesar Salad

Ingredient Quantity
Romaine Lettuce 1 head
Croutons 1 cup
Caesar Dressing 1/2 cup

Details

  • Prep Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 4

Steps

  1. Wash and chop the lettuce.
  2. Toss lettuce with croutons and dressing.
  3. Serve chilled with grated Parmesan cheese.

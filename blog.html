Entry 1: My First Steps in Programming

Date: November 26, 2024

Starting my programming journey has been both exciting and challenging. I began by learning basic HTML and CSS, which allowed me to create simple, static web pages. It felt amazing to see a page come to life from just a few lines of code!

Some of the things I found most helpful were online tutorials from ehealth elearning platform and push by my mentor in this journey Jamiu Musa. I also discovered how important it is to practice consistently and break down complex problems into smaller tasks.

Entry 2: Building My First Project

Date: December 1, 2024

After learning the basics, I decided to build my first project: a personal portfolio. It was a simple website where I showcased my skills and projects. I used HTML for structure, CSS for styling, and even experimented with a bit of JavaScript to add interactivity.

This project taught me the importance of planning and debugging. I faced challenges with layout and responsiveness but eventually figured out solutions through research and persistence. I'm proud of the outcome and look forward to building more complex applications.
